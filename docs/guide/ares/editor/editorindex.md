## How to log in

Log in to the official ==Ares Submission/Management System== (also known as the Submission System) of the corresponding journal using your **editorial board username**, which is typically the email address used to receive notification emails. 


If you forget your password, you can reset it by following the prompts on the website.

:::: steps
1. Click <Badge type="tip" text="Reset Password" />, you will receive an email from review@aressystem.org, containing a link to reset your password.

   ![aresemail](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A11.png)

2. Enter New Passwords and Log in again.
   
::::

::: note 
Generally, most domain email accounts can receive system emails successfully. If you have not received the relevant system emails, please view [Not receiving](#not-receiving-emails) emails for more suggestions.
:::

## How to switch roles

The ARES system will assign the role of **Academic Editor** to your account. An email notification will allow you to navigate to this role (below).

![A12](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A12.png)

## How to find manuscripts

Locate and process manuscripts in the role of an Academic Editor (For information on switching roles, please refer to [How to switch roles](#how-to-switch-roles)).

:::: steps
1. **Method 1:**

    Locate the manuscript in its possible state as instructed in the email.

2. **Method 2:** Quick Search
    
    Directly search for a manuscript ID using ::material-symbols:search-rounded /#0000ff:: Manuscript Search function in the navigation bar on the left side of the page.

    ![A15](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A15.png)

::::

## How to complete the COI checklist

As an academic editor, completing a conflict of interest (COI) checklist is an essential step to ensure the impartial review of manuscripts.

:::: steps
1. **Step 1**

    Locate and process manuscripts in the role of an Academic Editor (For information on switching roles, please refer to [How to switch roles](#how-to-switch-roles)).

2. **Step 2**

    Click <span style="color: skyblue;">Confilcts of Interest</span> to complete and submit the COI Checklist.

    ![A16](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A16.png)

## How to directly reject manuscripts

If the manuscript does not merit further review after the initial review, it may be directly rejected.

:::: steps
1. **Step 1**

    Change the number of **Review required to make decision** from ==3 to 0=={.danger}.

2. **Step 2**

   Click <span style="color: skyblue;">Preview and Make AE Recommendation</span> and you will be automatically redirected to the decision-making page to take action.

    :::

::::

## How to select reviewers

:::: steps
1. **Step 1**

    Click <span style="color: skyblue;">View Details</span> to review the manuscript content, select reviewers if the manuscript merits peer review.

    ![A19](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A19.png)

2. **Step 2**

    You can select suitable reviewers from the **Automatic Reviewer Suggestions from the Database** or add reviewers via **Create New Reviewers**.

    ![A20](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A20.png)

3. **Step 3**

    Invitation letters can be sent by clicking on <span style="color: red;">Invite</span> in the reviewer list. An invitation only takes effect once the invitation letter has been sent successfully.

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A21](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A21.gif)
    :::

## How to make recommendations

:::: steps
1. **Step 1**

    Click <span style="color: skyblue;">AE Recommendation</span> to review the detailed comments.

    ![A22](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A22.png)

2. **Step 2**

    Click on the <span style="color: skyblue;">Preview and Make AE Recommendation</span> button, select your recommendation, and enter your opinion on the manuscript.

## Not receiving emails

Generally, most domain email accounts can receive system emails successfully. If you have not received the relevant system emails, you may try the following methods:

1. Check the junk mail folder by searching **review@aressystem.org**
2. Switch browsers. Recommend Google or Firefox to log in to the account.
3. When using the browser, recommend selecting Incognito Mode.

   ![R19](/guide/ares/reviewer/fig/R19.png)
4. If your email security settings are too high, the above suggestions may not work. Please contact the managing editor via the journal's email address.
