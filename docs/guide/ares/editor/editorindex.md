## How to log in

Log in to the **ARES MS System** of the corresponding journal using your **editorial board username**.

If you forget your password, you can reset it.

:::: steps
1. **Step 1**: Click Reset Password, you will receive a reset link via email from <span style="color:#87CEFA">review@aressystem.org</span>.

::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
   ![A11](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A11.jpg)
:::

2. **Step 2**: Enter new passwords and log in again.

::: note 
If you cannot receive emails from the ARES system, please view [Not receiving emails](#not-receiving-emails) for more suggestions.
:::

## Not receiving emails

If you cannot receive the system emails, you may try the following methods:
1. Check the junk mail folder by searching: review@aressystem.org
2. Switch browsers. Recommend Google or Firefox browsers.
3. When using the browser, recommend selecting incognito mode.

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![R19](/guide/ares/editor/fig/R19.jpg)
    :::

4. If your email security settings are too high, the above suggestions may not work. Please contact the Managing Editor at the journal’s email address.

## How to complete the COI Checklist

As an academic editor, completing a Conflict of Interest (COI) Checklist is an essential step to ensure an impartial review.

:::: steps
1. **Step 1**: Process the manuscript as the **Academic Editor** (For information on switching roles, please refer to [How to switch roles](#how-to-switch-roles)).

2. **Step 2**: Click <Badge type="tip" text="Confilct of Interest" /> to complete and submit the COI Checklist.

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A16](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A16.jpg)
    :::

## How to directly reject manuscripts

If the manuscript does not merit further review after the initial review, it may be directly rejected.

:::: steps
1. **Step 1**: Change the number of <Badge type="tip" text="Reviews required to make decision" /> from **3** to **0**.

2. **Step 2**: Click <Badge type="tip" text="Preview and Make AE Recommendation" />, and you will be automatically directed to the decision-making page to take action.

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A17](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A17.jpg)
    :::

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A18andA182](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A18andA182.jpg)
    :::

::::

## How to select reviewers

:::: steps
1. **Step 1**: Click <Badge type="tip" text="View Details" /> to review the manuscript content.

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A25](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A25.jpg)
    ：：：

2. **Step 2**: Proceed to <Badge type="tip" text="Select Reviewers" /> to access the **Reviewer List**

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A19](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A19.jpg)
    ：：：

3. **Step 3**: You can select suitable reviewers from the **Automatic Reviewer Suggestions from the Database** or add reviewers via **Create New Reviewers**.

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A20](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A20.jpg)
    :::

:::note
When clicking <Badge type="tip" text="Search Full Database" />, you will be redirected to the **ARES Community** page, where you can search for and add more reviewers.

::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
![A261andA262](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A261andA262.jpg)
:::

4. **Step 4**: Invitation letters can be sent by clicking <Badge type="tip" text="Invite" />.

::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
![A21](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A21.jpg)
:::

::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
![A27](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A27.jpg)
:::

:::note
An invitation only takes effect once the invitation letter has been sent successfully.
:::

## How to make recommendations

:::: steps
1. **Step 1**: Click AE <Badge type="tip" text="Recommendation" />.

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A22](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A22.jpg)
    :::

2. **Step 2**: Check the detailed **Review Comments** by clicking “Accept/Minor Revision/Major Revision/Reject”. Select valid reviewers’ comments and click <Badge type="tip" text="Preview and Make AE Recommendation" />.

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A23](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A23.jpg)
    :::

3. **Step 3**: Make a recommendation, and enter your comments if necessary.

    ::: demo-wrapper img no-padding
    ![A24](/guide/ares/editor/fig/A24.jpg)
    :::
