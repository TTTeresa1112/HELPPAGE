## How to verify the successful upload of the files

:::: card
Once you click the <Badge type="tip" text="Submit" /> button, if files display in the "**Author Proofreading File(s)**" section, it indicates a successful upload.
::::

## How to notify the Editorial Office after uploading files

:::: card
No additional email notification is required. Upon successful upload of your file(s), the system will automatically notify the Editorial Office. The Assistant Editor will then process it promptly.
::::

## How to upload files exceeding 100 MB

:::: card
The system allows a maximum total file size of 100 MB per submission. If the total size of the files to be submitted exceeds this limit, please submit them in multiple batches.
::::

## How to address the system prompt "The current link is no longer valid, please retrieve it again!"

:::: card
Please contact the Assistant Editor who sent you the manuscript link via email for assistance in obtaining a new valid link.
::::
